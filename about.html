About

My name is Karrie Cheng. I was born in Hamilton, Ontario, but raised in Houston, Texas.

I've only really gotten into this whole programming thing in the past year. As a sophomore in college, I started developing a greater interest in it thanks to the unending positivity of a good friend and some neighbors. By the end of the year, I made the move to add Computer Science as a major despite being a semester away from graduating. I've been coding and enjoying myself ever since.

I like to participate in hackathons when I'm not working on my other personal projects. If you're big into hackathons, catch me at HackTX or TAMUHack this October.
