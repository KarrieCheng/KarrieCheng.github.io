About

My name is Karrie Cheng. I was born in Hamilton, Ontario, but raised in Houston, Texas.

I’ve only really gotten into this whole programming thing in the past year, despite some prior experience. As a sophomore in college, I started developing a substantial interest in it thanks to the unending positivivity from a good friend and some neighbors. By the end of the year I had heard enough to want to be a part of the excitement. Despite being a semester away from graduating, I made the move to add Computer Science as a major. I've been coding and enjoying myself ever since.

I like to participate in hackathons when I’m not working on my personal projects. If I had the time and money, I’d hit every hackathon— MLH or not. If you're big into hackathons, catch me at HackTX or TAMUHack this October.
